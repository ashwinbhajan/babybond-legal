# BabyBond AI — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 21 June 2026_

BabyBond AI ("the app", "we") helps you track fertility, pregnancy, and your baby's first years. This policy explains what we collect and how it is used.

## What we collect
- **Account information:** your email address and display name, used to create and sign in to your account (via Firebase Authentication, including optional Google Sign-In).
- **Health & wellness entries you choose to log:** cycle and period dates, symptoms, basal body temperature, pregnancy logs (weight, kicks, contractions, appointments), and baby logs (feeding, sleep, diapers, growth, vaccinations, milestones).
- **Photos** you add to memories or your profile.
- **AI chat messages** you send to the in-app assistant, used to generate responses.
- **Purchase information** (subscription status) processed by RevenueCat and the app stores. We never see your full payment details.
- **Device & diagnostics:** push-notification token, crash reports, and basic analytics (Firebase Crashlytics/Analytics) to keep the app reliable.

## How your data is used
Only to provide the app's features: syncing your entries across devices, personalising content to your journey stage, sending reminders you enable, and answering your AI chat questions. **We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties for advertising.** The app contains no ads.

## Where it is stored
Your data is stored with our service providers (Supabase for app data and photos, Google Firebase for authentication and notifications, RevenueCat for purchases) and is encrypted in transit.

## Your choices
- You can edit or delete individual entries at any time in the app.
- You can delete your entire account and its data: see [Delete your account](delete-account).
- You can disable notifications in system settings or in-app settings.

## Medical disclaimer
BabyBond AI provides general information and tracking tools only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare provider.

## Children
The app is intended for adults (18+) managing their own family journey and is not directed at children.
